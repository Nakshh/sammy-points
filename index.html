SAMMY Sigma Eta Points System

🧮 Timeframe: Weekly tracking. Points roll over during semester and reset at the end.
Baseline: Everyone starts at 0 points.
Positive = contributions.
Negative = missed duties or behavior issues.
Points can be spent on perks — currency resets to 0 when redeemed.
Excuses must be submitted via Google Form 24+ hrs before event (unless emergency).

📈 Positive Points
+5 – Attend Chapter (on time, full duration)
+2 – Attend Brotherhood Event (optional)
+5 – Attend Philanthropy Event
+3 – Attend Rush Event (optional)
+2 – Bring a Potential Rush
+2 – Help Set Up / Clean Event
+1/hr – Table During Rush
+2 – Tutor a Brother
+4 – Organize an Event (EC-approved)
+1 – Submit Exam Schedule or Transcript
+10 – GPA Increase ≥ 0.3
+10 – GPA Above 3.5

📉 Negative Points
-5 – Miss Chapter w/o Valid Excuse
-1 – Late to Chapter / Leave Early
-3 – Miss Mandatory Event
-3 – Sign Up to Help but No Show
-2 – Miss Rush Event w/o Excuse
-2 – Fail Committee Task
-5 – Doesn’t Participate in Rush Week
-5 – Disrespectful / Problematic Behavior
-1 – Ignore GroupMe @mentions (48+ hrs)
-5 – Lie About Attendance

💰 Points as Currency
6 pts – Brotherhood Dinner Buyout
8 pts – Skip Setup/Cleanup
6 pts – Skip Required Study Session
12 pts – Priority Big-Little Draft
30 pts – Rent House Room (1 night)
15 pts – Pick Brotherhood Activity
10 pts – Merch Discount ($10 off)

⚠️ Negative Balance Consequences
-5 pts – Formal warning
-10 pts – Barred from social
-15 pts – Exec review + no vote
-20 pts – Removal

Appeals in person at Chapter. System evolves at EC discretion.
